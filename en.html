Model architecture

A document platform built on Business Central

A compact walkthrough from the incoming document through validation to ERP, audit and operations.

  1. A document arrives

    E-mail, PDF or scan — in any format.

  2. The system extracts and validates

    It checks the data against ERP and preserves a complete audit trail.

  3. A person approves the exception

    People step in only when their judgment is needed.

  4. Validated data is posted

    Business Central, the warehouse and DMS receive the approved data.

This is a model architecture with illustrative parameters, not the result of a specific client project. Verified outcomes are listed in Case studies.
