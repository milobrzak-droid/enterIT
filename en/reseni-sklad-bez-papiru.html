Where it hurts today

Where the time really goes

Between the physical receipt and the record in the system there’s a lag of hours or days — the stock level lies.

Re-keying delivery notes means typos in quantities and codes.

Differences between the delivery note and the order surface late, once the supplier is no longer responding.

How the agent works

Step by step

A concrete flow — not promises. And just as important is what the agent deliberately does not do.

The worker photographs the delivery note

In a mobile app, or sends it to a designated address.

The agent extracts the data

Supplier, items, quantities, batches.

Matches it to the order

It compares: did what we ordered arrive? In the quantity ordered?

Books it in

In the ERP, and prepares the receipt note.

Reports differences at once and calls in a person

Missing items, surpluses and damage go straight to the buyer in Teams while the driver is still at the dock. Likewise, the agent doesn’t guess at an unknown item or an illegible delivery note.

What the agent does NOT do

Boundaries that build trust

Never on its own

It doesn’t book in goods that don’t match the order without the buyer’s confirmation.

Never on its own

It doesn’t decide on a claim for a damaged shipment with the supplier.

Never on its own

It doesn’t create new stock items without approval.
