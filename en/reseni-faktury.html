Step by step, from delivery to posting

A controlled flow with clear human review points.

Frequently asked questions

Does it work with Pohoda, Helios, K2 or Money S5?

Yes, we connect to common Czech and international ERP and accounting systems. If you use something else, get in touch — it’ll most likely work too.

What if the invoice is a phone photo or hard to read?

The agent can cope with lower-quality scans. Where it isn’t sure, it doesn’t guess the item, but sends the invoice to a person for review.

Do we have to change the way invoices reach us?

No. We connect to what you already use — an e-mail inbox, a supplier portal or a shared folder.

Who approves the invoice?

Always a person responsible for purchasing. The agent prepares everything for approval; it doesn’t pay or approve on its own.

What happens to an invoice the agent can’t match?

It flags it as an exception and hands it to the accountant for manual processing — nothing slips through unnoticed.
